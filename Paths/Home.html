ABOUT US

SSK Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a transport company engaged in the delivery of goods, with a reputation for accuracy and precision. We started our journey 15 years ago when Mr. Kuldeep Beniwal started his transport company from Haryana. We focused on timely delivery with agreed terms. This strategy was a huge success, and currently, we have a considerable fleet of trucks, and a professional staff. Our primary focus is timely delivery of goods. We provide the exact solution what our customers need. Our motto is to provide value addition to our customers’ business.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I be sured which vehicle is required for my shipments?

We always want to have our customer stress free due to which we have our customer support team which will resolve all your queries. You can reach out to our customer team via chat on Whatsapp or call on 8222859149

How can I book vehicle for my shipment?

We are providing multiple ways to get connected with us for booking the vehicle. You can raise a quote using our website or you can reach out to us on our Whatsapp number 8222859149 or can also make the call on the same number. We will guide you for the process and make your booking successful

How would I get the fare charges & bill amount?

Through the booking process, our customer representative will share all the details including distance, vehicle size and any other necessary information and after that the bill amount will be calculated. We will provide the best fare & charges which seems to be reasonable

Can I change the vehcle type after the booking process is completed?

Yes, You can change the vehicle type but there will be extra charges applicable on it

What are the available payment methods?

You can do the advance payment using IMPS/NEFT/RTGS and then need to pay the balanced amount after reaching at destination before unloading

Who will generate an e-waybill?

Consignor or Consignee before movement of goods needs to Generate Part A & B e-way bill. E-way Bill is not required for household items.

How Can I cancel the booking? Are there any cancellation fee?

You can cancel the booking by calling our customer care support team immediately. You booking will be cancelled as per the terms and conditions and there will be cancellation fee charged for it.
