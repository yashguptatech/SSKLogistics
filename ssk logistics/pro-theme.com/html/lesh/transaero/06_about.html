WE ARE SSK LOGISTICS!

SSK Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a transport company engaged in the delivery of goods, with a reputation for accuracy and precision. We started our journey 15 years ago when Mr. Kuldeep Beniwal started his transport company from Haryana. We focused on timely delivery with agreed terms. This strategy was a huge success and currently we have a considerable fleet of trucks, and a professional staff. Our primary focus is timely delivery of goods. We provide the exact solution what our customers need. Our moto is to provide value addition to our customers’ business. Today, we do not see ourselves as a traditional transport company. We are purely a modern service company with a considerable fleet of trucks. We offer a broad portfolio of services including transportation for all kinds of consumer goods such as consumer durables, electric and electronics goods, books and stationaries, merchandise, sports items and what not. We also expertise in delivery of heavy goods such as furniture, machineries, house materials and other items. Our capability to handle smallest to the heaviest materials makes us a unique player in transportation segment. Information and tacking are key to timely delivery of goods these days. We achieve this through our MIS and other modern communication tools such as instant messengers, emails, text messages etc.
